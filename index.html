About DJG


DJG is a local DJ company. DJG has been around for 4 years and have done over 50 parties since starting. These include Bar/Bat Mitzvahs, Engagement parties, school dances, birthday parties, and camp dances. We are capable of creating an energetic enviornment that can be tailored to all age groups.


Through experience, we have been able to create an effective system of planning events. With 2 in-person meetings, we get to know our clients, and learn what their likes and dislikes are. We are able to read the mood of the crowd and change the setting at a moments notice. We create a unique and different party for all of our clients, making sure no two parties are the same.
